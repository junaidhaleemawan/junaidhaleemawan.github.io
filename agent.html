How It Works

One agent, the workload of five intake staff.

A typical 5-person intake team handles initial responses, qualification questions, data entry into the CRM, and callback scheduling. This agent does all four, simultaneously, for every lead, instantly.

01 / RESPOND

Instant First Reply

Replies to every inbound enquiry — website, WhatsApp, or Meta ad — within seconds, any time of day.

02 / QUALIFY

Ask the Right Questions
